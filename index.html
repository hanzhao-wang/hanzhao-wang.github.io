About Me

Hello! My name is Hanzhao Wang and I am a Lecturer (Assistant Professor) in the Discipline of Business Analytics at the University of Sydney Business School.

I completed my Ph.D. at Imperial College Business School, Imperial College London, where I was advised by Prof. Kalyan Talluri and Prof. Xiaocheng Li.

Prior to Imperial, I received my master's degree in Operations Research from Columbia University and my B.S. degree in Central University of Finance and Economics.

Research Interests

  • Machine learning, large language models, generative AI for business, operations management.
